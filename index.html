Welcome

Grey Havens is a Scottish Deerhound kennel focused on preserving the historical breed according to the AKC standard. Our dogs can be found all over the United States and Canada. Many have had successful careers in both conformation showing and lure coursing sports. Whether you want a flashy show dog or a simple loving companion, we are committed to providing you with the perfect match. Discover why Sir Walter Scott dubbed the Scottish Deerhound . . .

“the most perfect creature of heaven”

Members in Good Standing:
> AKC Breeder of Merit
> Scottish Deerhound Club of America
> Mid Atlantic Deerhound Association
